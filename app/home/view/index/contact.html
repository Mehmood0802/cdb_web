Frequently Asked Questions
1.How to rent a power bank?

There is a QR Code on each machine. You can scan the QR Code by clicking "Scan QR" in the app. After that, you need to choose the payment method to recharge the deposit. Once you have made the deposit successfully, the power bank will pop out and you will be able to use it.

2.How to return the power bank?

Tuck the cables into the channel securely and insert the side of the power bank with metal chips into an empty socket till it reaches the bottom and stabilized.

3.What if l fails to make the payment?

Please check your balance of selected payment method or try other payment methods. If it fails again, please contact us (+65 82073520(Whatsapp) / contact@poweron.com.sg) to report the problem.

4.What if it fails to rent after a successful payment?

Your order will be cancelled automatically, no charges will be made. Please try to rent again or contact us (+65 82073520(Whatsapp) / contact@poweron.com.sg) to report the problem.

5.What if it shows as failed to return when I already returned the power bank into machine?

In this case, please contact us (+65 82073520(Whatsapp) / contact@poweron.com.sg) to report the problem. Our technicians will solve the problem as soon as possible.
